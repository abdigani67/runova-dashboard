# Market Validation — One-Page Summary
### AI Instagram DM automation for UK aesthetic clinics · £800 setup + £300/month

**Verdict: 🟡→🟢 MODERATE-to-STRONG.** Fundamentals are strong and evidence-backed; the two pillars the *pricing* depends on are unproven and must be validated before scaling. **Recommendation: enter a short validation phase, not straight to build.**

---

### The market is real and the timing is good
| Signal | Finding | Confidence |
|---|---|---|
| **Market size** | ~5,589 clinics, ~19,701 Botox/filler practitioners in the UK; **+437% in two years** | **High** |
| **Pain point** | Slow/missed enquiry response demonstrably loses bookings — backed by independent HBR (7× within 1hr) & MIT (5-min rule) research, not just vendor claims | **High** |
| **Tooling gap** | Leading PMS (Pabau, from £50/mo) has booking + email/SMS automation but **no native Instagram/social DM inbox** — a verified open slot | **High** |
| **Regulation** | England licensing (Aug 2025 consultation response) + Scotland law (passed Mar 2026) **professionalise & consolidate** the market — a tailwind toward serious, payable clinics | **High** |

### Two things are NOT proven — and they're the ones that set your price
| Risk | What's unproven | Confidence |
|---|---|---|
| **Instagram-first premise** | Every quantitative claim about Instagram's *share of enquiries* was refuted in verification. IG may not be the primary channel vs WhatsApp/phone/web | **Low** |
| **Willingness to pay** | Only the ~£50/mo PMS baseline survived. **£300/mo is ~6× that** — must be sold as ROI on recovered bookings, not as a tool line-item | **Low** |

> Refuted ≠ false. It means the *evidence* didn't survive scrutiny, so these must be confirmed with primary research before betting on them.

---

### Why it can still be a STRONG fit
The ROI math is favourable **if** Instagram is a real enquiry channel: one recovered Botox/filler booking is worth ~£150–£400+, so **£300/month needs only ~1–2 saved bookings to pay back.** That's a low bar — but it has to be tested with real clinics, not assumed.

### The validation plan (do in order; stop if step 1 fails)
1. **Interview 15–20 clinic owners** → where do enquiries *actually* land? (Resolves the Instagram gap.)
2. **Test £800 + £300/mo in those same calls** → frame as "1–2 recovered bookings." (Resolves the price gap.)
3. **Map 3–5 named competitors** (esteti, ClinicGrower, hyperleap) on price & IG-DM specificity.

**Decision gates**
- ✅ **GO / STRONG:** IG DM is a top-2 channel for most clinics **and** price tolerance holds → move fast; regulation + growth windows are open now.
- 🔄 **PIVOT:** Pain confirmed but enquiries are multi-channel → reposition as multi-channel enquiry automation (IG + WhatsApp + web).
- 💷 **RE-PRICE:** Strong interest, consistent recoil at £300 → test £150–£200 or per-booking pricing.
- 🛑 **NO-GO:** Clinics don't recognise losing bookings as a problem.
